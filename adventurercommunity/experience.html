We share the different sides of travel

From the Home page you saw The Big Buddha. You can visit the Hong Kong statue that symbolizes harmony between nature and human beings. It is such a mesmerizing sight. We guarantee it is worth the trip. Perfect place to take pictures, take in the fresh air with a view. Connecting with nature is also important for our health. After all that adventure do not forget to turn to the local markets. Walking through those markets, you find a variety of options to give your taste buds flavors to remember! You will not only fill your stomach with delicious food but also find different shops with all types of goods for those souvenirs we want to share with our loved ones of our trips. You will never be bored on the agenda is packed with interesting events that you cannot miss! From a firework show that can be enjoyed by all ages, Lion dance ambiance is always positive as well as surprising best enjoyed by friends and family. For those who like to learn about culture a Museum is also very enjoyable place you don't want to pass by. Adding Art. Visiting historic monuments provides a complete cultural experience. You must not let age or anyone hinder you from seeing, hearing, tasting, and living everything for yourself!

Tradition Activities

We practice one of many traditions in Thailand. We launch small candle lit boats into the water to make wishes and welcome happiness. It is practiced in different parts of Asia in their unique way with their historical background. It not only takes us to a childhood moment by making a paper boat but at the same time provides positivity that we share with everyone involved. It can be considered romantic as well for couples. We love to share magical moments that can be practiced wherever we may be to pass the hope and may it reach further.
