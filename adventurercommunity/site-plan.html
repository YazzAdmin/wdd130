Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to promote “Adventurers Community” a travel agency that envisions to challenge travelers perceptions as well as create a space for customers to share their experience.

Audience

The audience is intended for anyone looking for unforgettable experiences. Youth, adults and retirees. That means there is no age limit to live moments, create memories with family members, friends and making more connections.

Content

Home page

Envision a faraway place waiting for you to discover it. If you like the great outdoors imagine not just climbing mountains, site seeing from a distance or soaking in the sun. There is a vast land that few have set foot on with people waiting to share their culture. We encourage our community to live moments to the fullest, create bonds, and are certain you have not come across such a unique way of traveling. Let us show you that there are no limits! Leave fears behind. Whether it is alone, with family or friends. The fist step is stepping out of the comfort zone. After choosing the desired destination the transportation process will be a breeze. Sit back, relax, Leave the rest to us, and enjoy!

Images for the Home page

Experience

We share the different sides of travel. From the Home page you saw The Big Buddha. You can visit the Hong Kong statue that symbolizes harmony between nature and human beings. It is such a mesmerizing sight. We guarantee it is worth the trip. Perfect place to take pictures, take in the fresh air with a view. Connecting with nature is also important for our health. After all that adventure do not forget to turn to the local markets. Walking through those markets, you find a variety of options to give your taste buds flavors to remember! You will not only fill your stomach with delicious food but also find different shops with all types of goods for those souvenirs we want to share with our loved ones of our trips. You will never be bored on the agenda is packed with interesting events that you cannot miss! From a firework show that can be enjoyed by all ages, Lion dance ambiance is always positive as well as surprising best enjoyed by friends and family. For those who like to learn about culture a Museum is also very enjoyable place you don't want to pass by. Adding Art. Visiting historic monuments provides a complete cultural experience. You must not let age or anyone hinder you from seeing, hearing, tasting, and living everything for yourself! Tradition activities. We practice one of many traditions in Thailand. We launch small candle lit boats into the water to make wishes and welcome happiness. It is practiced in different parts of Asia in their unique way with their historical background. It not only takes us to a childhood moment by making a paper boat but at the same time provides positivity that we share with everyone involved. It can be considered romantic as well for couples. We love to share magical moments that can be practiced wherever we may be to pass the hope and may it reach further.

Memories

Spiritual connections. We have different type of destinations for each and everyone ready to explore with a tad of spirituality to complete the experience. Spirituality is good practice for mental health and we must always remember the importance of bonding. Here at adventurer community take a different route, we want to meet people. We have shared memories with monks and they have passed wisdom on to us. We are always happy to return to where we are happy!Memory lane. We have had amazing trips. We love spending quality time. Providing unlimited adventures. We daily receive overall experience from our community. We are happy to know we have surpassed expectations. Our goal is not only to guide you on your trip on the well-known places but to ensure we soak into the cultures, traditions, and take a different road to ensure unforgettable memories and maintain bonds that be will not forgotten. We invite you to share the memories that have been imprinted in your life. You can find us on social media Facebook and Instagram. We are committed to ensure you keep exploring!
